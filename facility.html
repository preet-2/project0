ABOUT US

Standing as an oasis of natural peace and hospitality at the foothill of a gigantic sedimentary hill, combining modern architecture with the domes and arches of a bygone era, is Green Heaven. Hidden in rustic and green setting are unique cottages, each depicting a chapter from the Great Indian Civilization.

ROOMS

All the rooms have balconies overlooking the blue water of magnificently designed swimming pool, green lawns and flowering shrubs, the quietness and the stillness of the surroundings accentuated by the chirping birds.The resort is built in 14 acres. To add to its beauty is surrounded by gigantic hill.

Banquet

The green environs and cool breeze, glittering lights of the rooms and garden on the still water of the swimming pool make a unique venue for special dinners, theme evenings hi-tea or sit down banquets
